Our Services

Construction And Renovation Solutions

Liquid and Dry Bulks Tanks Cleaning

Tank cleaning for liquid and dry bulks ensures safety and cargo purity through pressure washing, vacuuming, and chemical treatments. These methods prevent contamination and support compliance with industry standards.

Defective CCU´S repairs

Repairs of Cargo Carrying Units (CCUs) are vital for maintaining structural integrity and safety in offshore and onshore logistics. Welding, panel replacement, and corrosion control restore damaged containers to operational standards.

Electrical Services

Electrical services ensure safe power distribution across industrial and residential zones. Skilled technicians inspect, install, and maintain wiring, breakers, and control systems to prevent faults or outages. Compliance with electrical codes protects both personnel and infrastructure.

Pest Control and Furmigation

Pest control and fumigation eliminate health risks and protect facilities from infestations. Technicians apply targeted treatments to repel insects, rodents, or termites across urban and industrial sites. Fumigation uses controlled chemicals to disinfect enclosed spaces, ensuring zero tolerance for pests.

Plumbing Services

Plumbing services ensure safe water distribution and efficient wastewater removal across homes and industries. Specialists install, repair, and maintain systems like piping, valves, and fixtures, preventing leaks and water damage. Advanced techniques detect blockages and resolve flow issues, keeping infrastructure compliant and hygienic.

Supply of industrial and Welding Gases

Supplying industrial and welding gases supports manufacturing, fabrication, and energy sectors with critical resources. Technicians deliver and monitor cylinders or bulk tanks containing oxygen, acetylene, argon, and nitrogen—each tailored to specific operations.
